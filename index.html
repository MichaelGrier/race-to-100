How to Play Race to 100:

  • The game is designed for 2 players
  • In each turn, a player can roll the dice as many times as they want. Each roll get added to their CURRENT score.
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all of their CURRENT score gets lost, and their turn automatically ends.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold' at any point, which means that they end their turn, and their CURRENT score gets added to their TOTAL score.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on their TOTAL score wins the game
